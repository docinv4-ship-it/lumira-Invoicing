👤 About Us

Built to make invoicing feel simple.

Lumira Invoicing is a clean, fast invoice generator built for freelancers, small businesses, and teams that want a polished workflow without unnecessary friction.

What Lumira does

Lumira helps users create professional invoices in seconds. You choose a template, fill in the details, preview instantly, and export the final invoice as PDF or image.

The problem we solve

Invoicing tools often feel slow, cluttered, or locked behind sign-up walls. Lumira was designed to remove that friction and make the process feel smooth from the first click.

Who it is for

  • Freelancers
  • Small businesses
  • Agencies
  • Founders and solo operators

Why it feels different

The interface is built with a SaaS-style experience in mind: premium visuals, clear structure, live preview, and fast export options that feel modern and reliable.

Our vision

Lumira is the first step in a larger product ecosystem. The goal is to keep building simple, useful tools that save time and help people work smarter.
