The workflow stays short and clear.

Everything is designed to remove friction. The user never needs to think about setup, accounts, or hidden steps.

1

Open Lumira and choose a template

Users land on the website, open the template gallery, and pick a style that fits the brand. The template selection happens first so the form and preview are already aligned from the start.

2

Fill the invoice form

They enter business details, client details, invoice number, dates, item rows, tax, totals, notes, and payment info. Fields appear in a clean order so the workflow feels familiar and professional.

3

Watch the live preview update instantly

Every character added or removed in the form updates the invoice preview right away. No refresh button, no manual sync, and no confusion between form data and the template.

4

Download as PDF or image

When the invoice looks right, users export it as a PDF for sending or printing, or as an image for quick sharing. The output is clean, client-ready, and fast to generate.
