🔒 Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters.

Lumira Invoicing is designed to keep the experience simple and lightweight. We aim to collect only the data needed for website functionality, analytics, and product improvement.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect limited technical information such as browser type, device type, pages visited, and basic usage analytics to understand how the website is being used and to improve performance.

  • We do not intentionally sell personal data.
  • Invoice content may be stored locally in the browser or in connected services if that feature is enabled later.

2. Cookies and Analytics

We may use cookies or similar technologies for analytics, performance, and product improvement. If Google Analytics is enabled, it helps us understand traffic and feature usage.

  • Cookies may be used for session and preference purposes.
  • Analytics data may be collected by Google Analytics.
  • Future advertising tools may be added later to measure campaign performance.

3. Third-Party Services

Lumira Invoicing may rely on trusted third-party services such as Google Analytics, CDN providers, and hosting platforms to run the website and measure performance.

  • Google services may process usage data.
  • Hosting and CDN providers may log technical requests.
  • Any future ad tools or tracking pixels will be used only for measurement and product growth.

4. Data Protection

We take reasonable steps to keep data secure and use reputable infrastructure. However, no online service is completely risk-free, so we recommend not entering sensitive personal data unless required.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have the right to request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your data.
